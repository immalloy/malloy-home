## GameBanana Today release

Also yeah, [GameBanana Today](https://github.com/immalloy/gamebanana-today) exists now.

Site here: [immalloy.github.io/gamebanana-today](https://immalloy.github.io/gamebanana-today/)

It is a small browser for finding GameBanana mods released today, this week, or this month. You pick a game, look through recent mods, filter by category, search by name, and see little highlights like most downloaded, most liked, and most viewed.

![GameBanana Today game selector](/images/blog/gamebanana-today-selector.png)

![GameBanana Today mod list](/images/blog/gamebanana-today-mods.png)

The idea came from this GameBanana FNF post:

[FNF: GameBanana post](https://x.com/GameBananaFNF/status/2062723274421788887)

![The GameBanana FNF post that inspired GameBanana Today](/images/blog/gamebanana-today-inspiration-tweet.png)

It is actually very funny that I found out MythsList is the one running this account, since he is a very cool dude who has been in this fandom for a long time. I hope he can find his spark back.
